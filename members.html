Blackpink had their debut on August 8th, 2016 and within such a short period of time they broke many records with their albums getting recognised by many international fans. Currently, they are the most subscribed YouTube channels in history and also broke the record of most viewed music videos performed by any K-pop groups, making them to be the first K-pop Girl Band to reach Top Billboard 200. Their music is unique with special vibe that combines EDM and Pop music with some elements of Hip Hop and Rap, giving us energy boost and motivation for every young soul in the world.
